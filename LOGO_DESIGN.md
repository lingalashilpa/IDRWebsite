# IDR Logo Design Documentation

## Design Choices

### Icon
The logo uses a three-dimensional cube structure rendered in an isometric perspective. This geometric form symbolizes:
- **Structure and Stability**: The cube's rigid form represents the foundational frameworks and methodologies used in digital risk management.
- **Multi-faceted Risk**: Each visible face of the cube represents different dimensions of digital risk that IDR addresses.
- **Resilience**: The solid, interconnected structure conveys strength and the ability to withstand challenges.

### Color Palette
- **Orange (#FF6B35)**: The primary color represents energy, innovation, and forward-thinking approaches to digital risk. It conveys warmth and approachability while maintaining professional credibility.
- **Black (#000000)**: Provides strong contrast and represents security, authority, and technical expertise.
- **White (#FFFFFF)**: Ensures clarity and accessibility, representing transparency in risk management practices.

The gradient shading within the orange tones creates depth and dimension, making the logo more dynamic and modern.

### Typography
The accompanying text uses a bold, clean sans-serif typeface that ensures:
- Excellent legibility at all sizes
- Professional and contemporary appearance
- Clear association with technology and education sectors

### Scalability
The logo is designed to work effectively at both small (favicon, mobile header) and large sizes, with clear geometric lines that remain crisp and recognizable when scaled.
